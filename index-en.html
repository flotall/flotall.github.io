Welcome

Welcome to my personal webpage.

I am a PhD student in computer science under the supervision of Francis Lazarus at the laboratory G-SCOP of Grenoble in the Combinatorial Optimisation team.

The topic I study is part of PL (piecewise linear) geometry, and I am interesting more particularly to the algorithmic and combinatorial aspects. More precisely, I study PL isometric embeddings of polyhedral surfaces, that is faithful representations from a topological and metric viewpoint of surfaces obtained by gluing of Euclidean polygons.
